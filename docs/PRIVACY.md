# Privacy notice

GPTHub Tools processes files only to perform the operation selected by the user.

- No account is required.
- Input files are deleted after the job completes or fails.
- Result files are available through an unguessable capability link and are deleted within 60 minutes.
- File contents and original filenames are not included in application logs.
- IP addresses are transformed with a keyed hash before they are used for rate limiting.
- Upscaling and background-removal images are sent only to the AI endpoint configured by the operator. The bundled self-hosted deployment keeps those services on the private Compose network. Document, image-conversion, and PDF jobs are processed on the GPTHub Tools host.

Users should not upload files they do not have permission to process.
